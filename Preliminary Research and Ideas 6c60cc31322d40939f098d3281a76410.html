Basic Information About Topic (Introduction):

Tommy Douglas:

  • He was the premier of Saskatchewan (1)
  • He was the first leader of the NDP party (1)
  • He led the first socialist government in Canada (1)
  • He is recognized as the father of socialized medicine in Canada (1)
  • He was a Baptist minister in addition to a politician (1)

Main Research:

Timeline of Major Events:

  • In 1947 Tommy Douglas introduced the first universal hospital insurance programme in North America (4)
  • In 1959 Tommy Douglas announces his plan for universal publicly funded medical insurance coverage in Saskatchewan (4)
  • In 1960 organized medicine launches a campaign against Medicare (4)
  • In 1961 all of the 10 provinces were participating in the Hospital Insurance and Diagnostic Services Act (4)
  • In July of 1962, the Saskatchewan Medical Care Insurance Act takes effect, establishing universal publicly funded medical insurance for Saskatchewan residents (4)

Background and W5H:

  • In 1961 free healthcare is available to Canadians in all 10 provinces
